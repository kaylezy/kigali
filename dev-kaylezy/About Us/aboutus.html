About Us (What we do)

Remote Academy is an online platform offering top quality, education-to-employment programs and career coaching services for students worldwide. If you are looking for the next steps in your career, OpenClassrooms will get you there.

Our mission at Remote Academy is to provide a system that empowers motivated and talented people, regardless of background, age, experience, to succeed in their computer programming career and make education accessible to everyone, everywhere.


A new way to learn
